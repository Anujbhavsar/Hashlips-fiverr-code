About Us

Decentralized Raffle’s main goal is to provide a fun, decentralized and transparent way to conduct raffles for large prizes. Our aim is to award life changing prizes to the winners of our raffles, by providing full transparency and traceability.

Hashlips

This repo provides a nice and easy way for linking an existing NFT smart contract to this minting dapp. There are two ways of using this repo, you can go the simple route or the more complex one. The simple route is so simple, all you need to do is download the build folder on the release page and change the configuration to fit your needs.

Why Matic and not ETH?

We want to provide an inexpensive way for our community to enter our raffles, and unfortunately the gas fees from the Etherum Network do not allow us to do so. Most times, the gas fees are more expensive than the entry to our raffle. Using the Matic Network eliminates the super high gas fees for entering our raffles.

Easy Matic Network Set Up

Below are the easy steps required to add the Matic Network to your Metamask, transfer your ETH to Matic and buy Matic.

Adding Matic Network to Metamask:

  1. In your Metamask, go to the SETTINGS Page
  2. Click on Networks>Add Network
  3. Matic Mainnet
  • Network Name: Matic Mainnet
  • New RPC URL: https://polygon-rpc.com/
  • Chain ID: 137
  • Currency: MATIC
  • Bock Explorer URL: https://polygonscan.com/

Adding Matic Network to Metamask:

  1. Visit https://wallet.polygon.technology/
  2. Click “Polygon Bridge”
  3. Connect your Metamask Account (Etherum Mainnet). Ensure you have ETH
  4. Enter ETH you want transferred or “Bridged” to the Polygon blockchain (Matic)
  5. Click Transfer
  6. Transfer will take 5-10 minutes.
  7. Once transfer complete, switch your Metamask Network to “Matic Mainnet
  8. Your new Matic will be available

Buying Matic and sending to your Metamask:

  1. Purchase Matic at any Crytpo marketplace that carries it (like Coinbase)
  2. One Matic is in your marketplace account, copy your Matic Wallet address from Metamask.
  3. Send the Matic from your Crypto Marketplace account to your Metamask (wallet address you copied
